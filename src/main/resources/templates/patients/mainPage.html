What problem does it solve?

Patients find it difficult to reach healthcare locations in-person due to long queues and anxiety-inducing environments.

How is this problem currently solved?

Patients currently go to the clinic, wait for a ticket, deal with ticket queues, phone receptionists, and rely on Google or word of mouth for information.

How will this project make life better?

HealthAce eliminates annoying hospital lines and reduces hospital anxiety by providing online appointments, health record management, and easy access to health services.
